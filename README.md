## 🧘 Key Features

### 🌟 Three Specialized Practices
- **Shanti (Calm)**: Cultivating inner stillness through gentle, rhythmic breathing.
- **Dharana (Focus)**: Enhancing concentration by steadying your attention through the breath.
- **Nidra (Sleep)**: A gentle descent into restful sleep with calming, long-exhale patterns.

### 🎧 Immersive Audio Guidance
- **Speech**: A gentle, soothing voice guides you through each phase (Inhale, Hold, Exhale).
- **Pink Noise**: Dynamic soundscapes that swell as you breathe in and dissolve into a peaceful pause.
- **Pure Silence**: For those who prefer to practice in the quiet of their own mind.

### 🌓 Desktop Mode
Shwaas adapts instantly when you rotate your device. **Desk Mode** transforms the interface into a beautiful, minimal layout perfect for your nightstand or workspace, keeping the time visible while giving the breathing animations room to expand.

### ✨ Rich Visual Experience
- **Mandala Animations**: Dynamic, fluid shapes that sync with your breath.
- **Haptic Feedback**: Subtle physical pulses that guide your practice without needing to look at the screen.
- **Dynamic Themes**: Choose between Light and Dark modes to match your environment.

---

## 🛠 Technology Stack

- **SwiftUI**: Core UI framework for a modern, responsive interface.
- **AVFoundation**: Advanced audio management for voice and environment sounds.
- **CoreHaptics**: Precise haptic feedback for a mindful tactile experience.
- **Vision**: Integrated for future-ready visual processing.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. Open **Shwaas.swiftpm** in Swift Playgrounds on Mac or iPad.
2. Run the app and follow the onboarding to choose your practice.
3. Use the **Settings** to customize your theme, haptics, and audio preferences.
4. Try rotating your device to experience **Desk Mode** during a session.

---

## 📜 Philosophy
Shwaas (Sanskrit: श्वास) means "Breath". In ancient yogic traditions, the breath is the bridge between the body and the mind. By consciously regulating the breath, we can influence our nervous system, reduce stress, and achieve a state of presence. Shwaas is designed to be your companion on this journey.
